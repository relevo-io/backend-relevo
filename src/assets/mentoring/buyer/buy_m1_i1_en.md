# Module 1: Define buying objectives

Welcome to the first module of the Buyer Route. Acquiring an active business is an excellent way of entrepreneurship.

## Define your criteria before starting:

- **Sectors of interest:** In which sectors do you have experience or feel comfortable managing?
- **Geographical location:** Do you prefer a local business to be hands-on or are you looking for something managed remotely?
- **Budget and financing:** How much of your own capital do you want to invest and how much external financing will you need?

> **Tip:** Having well-defined search criteria saves time and allows you to focus your efforts only on offers that truly fit your profile.
