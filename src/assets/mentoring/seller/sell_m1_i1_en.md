# Module 1: Prepare the company for sale

Welcome to the first module of the Seller Route. Deciding to transfer your business is a very important milestone.

## Key aspects to consider:

- **Clear reasons:** Buyers will always ask why the business is being sold (retirement, change of sector, health, etc.). Transparency builds trust.
- **The right timing:** Analyze whether the company is going through a stage of stability or growth to maximize its value.
- **Avoid common mistakes:** Do not neglect the day-to-day activity during the sales process and maintain maximum confidentiality.

> **Tip:** Before publishing any offer on Relevo, make sure you have a clear idea of the transition you would like to make.
